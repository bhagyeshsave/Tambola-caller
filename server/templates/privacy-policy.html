Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 21, 2026

Welcome to Tambola Caller. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Tambola Caller is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • No Personal Information: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.
  • No Account Required: You can use the app without creating an account or signing in.
  • Local Data Only: All game session data (generated numbers, settings) is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

How We Use Information

Since we do not collect personal information, we do not use, share, or sell any user data. The app functions entirely offline and locally on your device.

Data Storage

Game session data and preferences are stored locally on your device using secure local storage. This data:

  • Remains on your device only
  • Is not accessible to us or any third parties
  • Can be cleared by uninstalling the app or clearing app data

Third-Party Services

Tambola Caller does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. Your usage of the app is completely private.

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children or any other users.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
